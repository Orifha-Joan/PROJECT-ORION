# Orion

> *An AI Chief of Staff built first for myself, then for everyone else.*

## Why Orion Exists

In 2024, I made the decision to fully transition into technology.

Since then, I have completed courses, earned certifications, watched countless videos, and built several projects. While each of those experiences taught me something valuable, they all shared one thing in common.

Once they were finished, they became static.

They were projects built to complete a course, satisfy a tutorial, or demonstrate a concept. They solved someone else's problem, not mine. After documenting them, they rarely became part of my daily life.

Eventually, I reached a point where I realized I wasn't lacking knowledge.

I was lacking ownership.

I didn't need another certificate. I needed something that I would wake up every day and rely on.

That realization became Orion.

---

## The Problem

Every day I consume information.

I read articles.

I watch videos.

I learn about cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, leadership, business, productivity, and countless other topics.

Yet every morning I still ask the same questions.

* What should I work on today?
* What should I learn today?
* What should I write about?
* Which opportunity deserves my attention?
* Am I actually making progress?

Instead of building momentum, I spend valuable time deciding what to do next.

As someone who wants to grow a career in AI, cybersecurity, and consulting, this is the wrong problem to have.

---

## Why I Am Building Orion

Orion is my AI Chief of Staff.

Its purpose is not simply to automate tasks.

Its purpose is to help me make better decisions, remain consistent, and continuously build.

Orion will organize my work, prioritize my goals, prepare me for meetings, suggest what I should learn, generate content ideas, and remind me to keep building.

It is designed to remove decision fatigue so I can focus on creating value.

Every feature must solve a real problem that I personally experience.

If I would not use the feature every week, it should not exist.

---

## Version 1.0

Orion v1.0 will provide:

* A personalized morning briefing.
* Intelligent task prioritization.
* Calendar awareness.
* Meeting preparation.
* Daily learning recommendations.
* Draft LinkedIn posts based on my work and interests.
* Project progress tracking.
* Gentle accountability that encourages consistent building.

Orion should become the first application I open every morning.

---

## Beyond Productivity

Orion is more than a productivity application.

It is evidence.

Evidence that I can identify a real problem, design an architecture, build cloud-native software, integrate artificial intelligence, deploy production-ready systems, and continuously improve them.

It represents the transition from consuming knowledge to creating products.

---

## The Long-Term Vision

Today, Orion serves one person.

Tomorrow, it should be capable of serving many.

The architecture, engineering decisions, documentation, and development process should reflect that ambition from the very beginning.

Every version should move closer to a platform that helps professionals organize their work, think more clearly, and consistently create value.

---

## Engineering Principles

* Build products, not tutorials.
* Solve real problems before interesting problems.
* Ship something every week.
* Every feature must save time or improve decisions.
* AI is a teammate, not the product.
* Consistency beats intensity.
* Build first. Learn what is necessary along the way.

---

## Personal Commitment

I am not building Orion to complete another project.

I am building Orion to become the type of engineer and consultant I want to be.

Every deployment is a step toward that goal.

Every sprint is progress.

Every improvement compounds over time.

Orion is not my destination.

It is the vehicle that will take me there.
